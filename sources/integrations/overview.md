# What is an integration?

Integrations are used to connect your Shippable CI or CD workflows to third party platforms and services. We believe in separating authentication credentials from your yml definitions for better security and privacy.

Using an integration is a 3 step process:

* Add an integration to your account. To do this, go to the gear menu in your top navbar and click on `Integrations` in the sidebar menu
